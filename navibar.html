THEME

Humans have been kicking around a pale blue dot in space for a while now. The rise and fall, the ups
 and downs, and the meandering turns humankind has taken have been painted and weaved onto the 
tapestry - our legacy and the proof of our existence in an otherwise unchanging and indifferent universe.
 People spanning cultures, eras, nationalities, and backgrounds have contributed to compiling the saga 
of all time. This saga has been visualized, part by part, and time-to-time in tapestries. Join us as we weave 
together the tapestry of the lives, the ideas, and the revolutions made by transient individuals to make an
 immortal and magnificent representation of humanity: Humanity's Tapestry.
